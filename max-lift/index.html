Privacy Policy

By using the Application, You agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Interpretation and Definitions

Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Application refers to Max Lift, the mobile application provided by the Company.
  • Company refers to Max Lift.
  • Device means any device capable of running the Application, including a mobile phone or tablet.
  • Service refers to the Application.
  • You means the individual using the Application.

Information We Collect

Information Collected by the Application

Max Lift does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Date of birth

The current version of the Application does not provide user accounts, cloud synchronization, workout history storage, analytics tracking, or crash reporting services.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The Application may store preferences and settings locally on Your Device to provide functionality and improve Your experience.

This information may include:

  • Application preferences
  • User settings
  • Configuration options

On Android devices, this information may be stored using encrypted local storage mechanisms. On iOS devices, certain information may be stored securely using the Apple Keychain.

This locally stored information remains on Your Device and is not sold to third parties.

Advertising Data

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Application interaction information
